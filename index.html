业余时间干啥不空虚？ What shoule be done to avoid bore? 2022/9/18

业余时间主动输出，例如画画，写文章；会比被动接受，例如刷抖音，看书，听音乐更有自我效能感。这容易触发心流，更有效地改善人的心情。

Doing productive activities in free time, such as drawing, writing, is more self efficacy than just acceptance, such as watching tiktok, reading books or listening to music.

Creating something could trigger flow state, which can improve our mentaility more effectively.
